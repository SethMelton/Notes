# Summer Salad

### Dressing:

2 Tbsp Honey

2Tbsp Lime Juice

1-2 Tbsp EVOO

Pinch of Salt

#### Salad:

2 cups Tri-Color Cherry Tomatoes (Halved)

2 cups Cocktail Cucumbers (Sliced)

2 cups Watermelon (cut into cubes)

12 Basil leaves (Chopped)

12 fresh Mint leaves (chopped)

1/2 cup Feta (crumbled)
